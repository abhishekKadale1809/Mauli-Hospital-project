    Frequently Asked Questions

    We’re here to support you with trusted information and answers to your concerns.

    What services are provided in your maternity department?

    Our maternity department offers prenatal care, labor and delivery services, C-section delivery, postnatal care, lactation support, and parenting education to ensure safe and comfortable childbirth experiences.

    Can I choose my gynecologist or obstetrician?

    Yes, you can choose your preferred gynecologist or obstetrician from our team, subject to availability. We encourage early booking during pregnancy for continuity of care.

    What are the visiting hours for mothers and newborns?

    Visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM. To ensure patient rest and safety, only close family members are allowed during visits.

    Do you provide vaccination and pediatric follow-ups for newborns?

    Yes, our pediatrics department provides complete vaccination programs, regular growth monitoring, and specialized child care services from birth through adolescence.

    What facilities are available in the NICU (Neonatal Intensive Care Unit)?

    Our NICU is equipped with advanced incubators, ventilators, and 24/7 neonatal specialists to care for premature or critically ill newborns.

    Is breastfeeding support available in the hospital?

    Absolutely. Certified lactation consultants are available to guide new mothers on breastfeeding techniques and overcome initial challenges.
